Password Strength Categories: Understanding and Categorizing Security Levels

Passwords fall into categories based on strength: Weak (short, simple), Medium (moderate length, some variety), Strong (long, diverse characters), and Very Strong (high entropy, all types). Our password strength checker evaluates your generated password against these categories, providing insights to improve security. Use this to avoid common pitfalls like reusing passwords across sites.
