Step-by-Step Guide: How to Calculate Age from Birthdate

  1. Subtract the birth year from the current year to get base age.
  2. Adjust if the current month/day is before the birth month/day (subtract 1 year).
  3. Calculate months: (current month - birth month) + 12 if negative.
  4. For days: Use milliseconds difference divided by 86400000.
  5. For seconds: Multiply days by 86400.
  6. Use our exact age calculator for instant results!

What is Age Calculation? In-Depth Definition and History

Age calculation refers to determining the time elapsed since a birthdate, expressed in various units like years, months, days, or seconds. The History of Age Calculation

Age tracking originated in ancient civilizations for taxation and military service. The Julian calendar in 45 BC improved accuracy, evolving into the Gregorian in 1582. Today, digital tools like our online age calculator use these systems for precise computations, including how to calculate age in seconds for scientific purposes.

Age Calculation Formula Breakdown

Core formula: Age in years = Current Year - Birth Year (with month/day adjustment). For months: (Years × 12) + (Current Month - Birth Month). Days: (Current Date - Birth Date) in milliseconds / 86400000. Seconds: Days × 86400. These power every exact age calculator, ensuring reliability.
