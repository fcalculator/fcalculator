Basic World Population Calculator: Project Population and Needs

Enter a year (1925-2125) and select a country to view population data, growth rate, and projected needs. Formula: P(t) = P0 * e^(r*t), where r ~0.85% annual growth (adjusted for decline post-2084).

Example: For World in 2050 = ~9.66 billion (UN projection). Needs: Food demand +100-110%, water shortages for billions. Use for sustainability planning.
