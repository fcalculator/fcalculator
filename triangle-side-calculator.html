Formulas for Right Triangles

Key formulas include:

  • Pythagorean Theorem: c = √(a² + b²) – find hypotenuse from legs.
  • Trigonometric Functions: sin(α) = a/c, cos(α) = b/c, tan(α) = a/b – calculate angles or sides.
  • Area: (1/2) * a * b – simple for right triangles.
  • Inradius: (a + b - c)/2 – fits inside the triangle.
  • Circumradius: c/2 – circle through vertices.
  • Medians: From vertices to midpoints, visualized in radar chart.
