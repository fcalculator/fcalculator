Inner Workings of the 3-Minute Countdown System

This timer employs JavaScript to ensure precise decrements every second, initiating from 3 minutes as the page renders. The operational flow comprises:

  1. Establishing the starting seconds at 180 (3 minutes).
  2. Activating setInterval to subtract one second at 1000-millisecond intervals.
  3. Computing and displaying minutes alongside padded seconds.
  4. Terminating the loop upon reaching zero.
  5. Offering a restart mechanism that restores the initial time and recommences the process.

Fundamental equations: minutes = Math.floor(time / 60); seconds = time % 60, with zero-padding for single digits. Executed entirely client-side, it operates independently of networks, compatible with standard web environments.
