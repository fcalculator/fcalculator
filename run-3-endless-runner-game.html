How the Run 3 Endless Runner Game Works

This interactive 3D game replicates the core mechanics of Run 3, where players control a character running through procedurally generated space tunnels. The tunnels feature random gaps and obstacles, requiring precise jumps and gravity shifts to avoid falling into the void. Power cells appear randomly for collection, enabling character unlocks with unique traits like higher jumps or faster speeds. The game accelerates over time, challenging players' timing and spatial awareness.

To begin, select an unlocked character from the menu and enter infinite mode. The physics engine simulates momentum, gravity rotations, and collisions, providing an educational tool for understanding 3D transformations and vector mathematics.

Understanding Game Mechanics and Physics

The gameplay revolves around vector-based movement and gravitational shifts. Jumping follows a physics model with initial velocity and deceleration:

\[ v_y = v_0 - g \cdot t \] \[ y = y_0 + v_0 t - \frac{1}{2} g t^2 \]

Where \( g \) represents simulated gravity. Gravity shifts involve rotating the player's reference frame by 90 degrees, allowing wall-running, which teaches coordinate transformations in 3D space.

Character Abilities and Unlocks

Each character offers distinct physics parameters: Runner (balanced), Skater (faster but lower jump), Lizard (high jump, slower), Bunny (double jumps). Unlocks occur via collected power cells, encouraging repeated plays and strategy adaptation.

Step-by-Step Guide to Playing

1. From the menu, choose an unlocked character (start with Runner).

2. Click 'Start Infinite Mode' to begin running through tunnels.

3. Use left/right arrows to maneuver between tunnel lanes.

4. Press up arrow to jump over gaps; hold for longer jumps with certain characters.

5. Spacebar shifts gravity to adjacent walls, enabling new paths.

6. Collect glowing power cells to increase your total and unlock new characters.

Advanced strategy: Anticipate tunnel rotations and gaps by calculating distances visually, as speed increases every 1000 meters.

FAQ

How do gravity shifts work? Pressing space rotates your character's gravity to the nearest wall, allowing seamless transitions between tunnel surfaces.

What are power cells for? They accumulate across runs; reach thresholds like 300 to unlock the Skater for enhanced speed.

Is there a level system? This version focuses on infinite mode with procedural challenges, but distance milestones simulate level progression.

How to handle increasing difficulty? As speed ramps up, focus on predictive jumping and gravity flips to navigate wider gaps.
